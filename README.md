## What is this?

The Solana Faucet gives away free SOL to anyone who asks for it.

Paste your wallet address. Click claim. Receive 0.001 SOL. That's it.

No accounts. No gas fees. No tricks. Just a wallet address and a button.

---

## Why?

In 2010, Gavin Andresen built the Bitcoin Faucet. It gave away 5 BTC per visitor — over **19,700 BTC** total. At today's prices, that's more than a billion dollars. It was the first way most people ever touched Bitcoin. It helped launch an entire movement.

We're doing the same thing for Solana.

Getting your first crypto is still harder than it should be. You need to find an exchange, pass KYC, link a bank account, and wait. By the time you're through, the curiosity is gone. The Solana Faucet removes every barrier. Download a wallet, copy your address, claim. Two minutes. Zero cost.

Every person who gets their first SOL here might become the next builder, trader, or contributor to the Solana ecosystem. That's worth more than 0.001 SOL.

---

## Two Faucets, One Mission

The site ships with two interfaces:

**Modern Faucet** — Clean, minimal, built for 2025. For people who just want their SOL.

**Original Faucet** — A faithful recreation of the 2010 Bitcoin Faucet layout. Same sidebar, same form fields, same spirit. We kept it because it's crypto history and it deserves to be remembered.

Both are identical in function. Pick the one you like.

## The $FAUCET Token

The faucet is funded by the **$FAUCET** token on Solana. Trading volume and fees flow directly back into the faucet wallet, keeping it alive. Every trade helps someone get their first SOL.

The token has not launched yet. Follow [@TheSolanaFaucet](https://x.com/TheSolanaFaucet) for launch updates.

## How to Claim

1. **Get a wallet** — Download [Phantom](https://phantom.app) or [Solflare](https://solflare.com). Free. 30 seconds.
2. **Copy your address** — Your wallet gives you a Solana address. Tap to copy it.
3. **Claim** — Paste your address on the faucet and hit claim. SOL arrives in seconds.
